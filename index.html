I’m a digital product designer living in Brooklyn. I care deeply about bettering human lives, questioning the technology that we’ve put into our day-to-day experience, and enhancing the mood in any team.

I'm currently leading a design team at DigitalOcean. The developer space is filled with tools that are neither intuitive nor fun to use. We flip that and push towards creating the best solution rather than a solution.

I've been interested in making human lives better for a while. I've collaborated with Oliver Farshi on a number of projects to push this forward.

Nokia is a company as old as the tech industry. I joined a team to create a very interesting adventure camera.
