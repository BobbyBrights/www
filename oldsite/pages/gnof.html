GNOF

The Greater New Orleans Foundation (GNOF) has been a mainstay in New Orleans for over thirty years, and was instrumental in helping revitalize the city after Hurricane Katrina. I collaborated with fellow designer Oliver Farshi to breathe life into an aging web portal. We worked closely with their passionate team to further enable philanthropic efforts in the region.
