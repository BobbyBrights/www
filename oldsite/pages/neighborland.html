Neighborland

In an effort to facilitate individuals in their communities, cofounders Dan Parham, Tee Parham, and Candy Chang created Neighborland. The platform has enabled people across the country to start conversations that have turned into grassroots movements overnight. Neighborland helps to connect community members to causes they are passionate about changing.
