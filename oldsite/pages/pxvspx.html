PX vs PX

Eileen Tjan and I worked with animator Victor Uhal to create an online competition for designers. Happening in early 2015, the competition will be streamed live via Google Hangouts On Air and will feature six contestants from around the world. This will give spectators a rare look into the process of talented designers.
